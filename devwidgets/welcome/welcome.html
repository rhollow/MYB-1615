Unifying the online experience at UC Berkeley

My Berkeley is a new resource for the UC Berkeley community. In this Preview release, students can create groups, create and share information, manage important tasks, receive messages from school administrators, and participate in a network of classmates, colleagues and other Berkeley students.

 We are hard at work improving MyBerkeley for our August 2011 release! Until then you may notice some broken functionality, and some data in the application (such as your names) may appear garbled. This is all in preparation for a new, improved MyBerkeley in August, so stay tuned!
