Welcome to CalCentral Pilot.

CalCentral is a pilot program designed to bring many campus online services under one roof over time.

What you should know before joining this pilot:

  • You may receive multiple messages for the same task or event.
  • Messages may be deleted at any time due to system or project events.
  • The design and features presented may change at any time.
  • By participating in this pilot you may be invited to participate in surveys and focus groups to help us determine the effectiveness of the design and features presented here.
  • Some settings allow some or all of the CalCentral information about you to be found and viewed by anyone in the world. Please edit your sharing settings and/or your content with this in mind.
  • This system is in active development. While it has been tested and is believed to be ready for your use, in the course of improving this pilot some data loss may occur. Please make backups of all documents and content that you create or share in CalCentral
